Construction Projects

A few projects I have lead as a Constuction Superintendent



Spiritwood Retirement Homes

A collection of 16 retirement homes, Spiritwood Cottages is the model of modern building practices. Utilization of latest methods we were able to build these units with shared walls that were acoustically dampened ensuring tenants a quiet space. Furthermore these building were built to meet the strict building codes of Issaquah WA and due to their location in the wetlands they required consistent communication and oversight from multiple Government agencies.

ERW Lighting

A 10,000 square foot commercial remodel, this building was constructed in 1940 and required extensive remodeling. It now houses ERW Lighting a promentant light solution supplier who requested that we strip the buidling down fully and create a modern and creative space utilizing the most advance lighting options on the market. These fixtures all operated via Athena and were capable of inter light and sensor communications. They are designed to respond to user input ranging from cell phones to as simple as entering the building.

Staff Meal Eatery

A smaller project only at 800 square feet roughly, this building required significant structural retrofits to be permitted for occupancy. Per the clients request we procured aged finishes and materials from refuse yards to give it an aged appearance. Don't let appearances fool you. The inner workings of this building are all up to spec and required intuitive installation methods to blend the two components.
